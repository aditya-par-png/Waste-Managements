How to Use This App

1. View your pickup schedule: Navigate to the Schedule page to see your upcoming waste pickup dates and times.

2. Request a new pickup: On the Home page, fill out the pickup request form, select your waste type, date, and time, and submit the request.

3. Payment Information: After your waste has been collected, payment can be made on-site in cash. Please ensure you have the correct amount ready at the time of pickup.

4. Stay Updated: You will receive notifications for scheduled pickups, so stay updated on your waste management status.
